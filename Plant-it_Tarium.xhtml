Pilea. Pilea peperomiodes. The Pilea, also known as the Chinese Money Plant is known for it's circular "coin-like" leaves.

Plant Care

  • Bright indirect sunlight
  • Chunky aeraited soil
  • Water frequently

Pros

  • Easy to propagate
  • Heardy
  • Many pups/baby plants
  • Non-Toxic to cats and dogs

Cons

  • Can drop leaves if there is not enough sunlight
  • Prone to root-rot
  • Dramatic
